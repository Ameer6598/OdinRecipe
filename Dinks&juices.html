Mint Margarita Recipe

    Ingredients

  • Water 1 cup

  • Sugar 1 tbsp or to taste (option)

  • Mint springs 10-12

  • Juice of 3 Lemons

  • Salt/black salt 1 pinch

  • Black pepper 1 pinch (optional)

  • 7up/Sprite half liter

  • Ice

  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  • In a blender, add water, sugar, mint springs, lemon juice, salt/black salt, black pepper, and ice. Blend until incorporate.
  • Add mixture equally in 3 glasses. Add ice, pour 7up/Sprite.
  • Garnish margarita with mint leaves and serve immediately.
